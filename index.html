Graduated! Class of 2001, BA in Computer Science!

  Welcome to my homepage at Boston University. I have already graduated with a Bachelors of Arts in Computer Science and have moved back to Chicago, Illinois.

  I am currently a graduate student at Depaul University working on my Masters of Science in Information Systems at the Depaul CTI. I should be done by 2006. I also have a website there.

  My new site is at spong.org. I have my blog there. You can also view my graduation pictures there too.
